Our Mission

At Happy Paws, our mission is to provide loving homes for animals in need. We believe every animal deserves a second chance at life and a forever family.

About Us

Founded in 2010, Happy Paws Animal Adoption Center is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming abandoned, abused, and stray animals. We work tirelessly to provide shelter, medical care, and love to our furry friends.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff is passionate about animal welfare. From veterinarians to caretakers, each member plays a vital role in ensuring the well-being of our animals.

How You Can Help

You can support our cause by adopting, donating, or volunteering. Every contribution helps us save lives and give animals the love they deserve.
